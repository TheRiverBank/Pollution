In this project, pollution is discussed with an emphasis on air pollution. I will look at the main causes of pollution and the steps that are being taken to decrease the amount of toxic gases that are being released. This will include going from nonrenewable sources of energy to renewable ones.

This plot shows how many million tonnes of greenhouse gasses that were released from 1990-2016, measured in the equivalent amount of carbon dioxide that would produce that same amount of energy. Electricity seems to be a big contributor to greenhouse gases. Of the 102812 tonnes of greenhouse gases, electricity and heat makes out to be 30% of the total emission.
